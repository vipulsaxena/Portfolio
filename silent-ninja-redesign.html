Silent Ninja is a call blocking application suited for meetings, work hours, movies and many other occasions. It has smart filters to identify spam vs [imp]repetative calls and options to auto-respond to a incoming call via sms on user-concent.

I collaboratively worked with a Senior product designer in designing the 1st version of the app and then later in 2014 took a re-take to improvise the visual design and UX of the app. What you see below is a compilation of simplified key views of the latest app.

Simplified UX: Slidable list-items with most prefered actions and a 2 tab layout- Recent & Blacklist
