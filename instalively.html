Background

InstaLively is a mobile app which can directly LiveStream (one to many broadcast) from any mobile phone. It seamlessly integrates with one's Youtube channel, thereby managing all the DRM (Digital Rights Management) and enabling Video Monetization. InstaLively lets you auto-post the live video onto your social networks with inline video viewing.

We focus on Instant Video/ Audio Digitization on low 3G bandwidths using just a Mobile app & thus Making it as simple and cost-effective as possible for the everyday enterprise to create/ broadcast content from any location, thereby democratizing Online Video

In early 2015, the age of Meerkat(the app) and Periscope, I started to work on this live streaming application as a independent Freelancer and later became part of the founding team. My role in this project was that of a Design Technologist -Leading the Design and Front-End development for the mobile and web app.

Problem Statement

Why isn't there a way to see what's happening right now, anywhere in the world? There are over billion smartphone owners have already shown an immense affinity to share photos and videos, the cameras on these phones are still an underutilized resource. That if there was a way to turn this supply into a distributed, on-demand workforce?


The Approach

Youtube Live Streaming API's comes to the resuce. We created a cross-platform wrapper app using Youtube livestreaming API for mobile(Android and iOS) and Web using MEAN stack.
